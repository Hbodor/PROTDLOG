The game is played on a 5x12 board, where the player possesses chess pieces and has to defend their side of the board from the attacks of checker pieces attacking from the right side. The player starts with only the king piece in their disposition as well as 100 coins that allow the player to buy other pieces and place them where they choose. They can move their pieces around the board. They can also buy or sell pieces in the store throughout the game. Checkers start attacking from the right side and their aim is to reach to left side where they can damage the king's health. Chess pieces automatically attack visible checkers using bullets and the player is rewarded in coins for every checker piece killed.
